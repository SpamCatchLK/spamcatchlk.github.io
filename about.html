Welcome to Spam Catch Sri Lanka, a revolutionary project dedicated to protecting your privacy and combatting the exploitation of your personal data. We believe in a world where technology empowers and enables us, rather than being used to profit from our information and compromise our privacy.

In today's digital age, the unauthorized sale of contact details has become a rampant issue, leading to an overwhelming amount of unsolicited advertising and spam. We understand the annoyance and frustration this can cause, and we firmly believe that it is our collective responsibility to stand against such practices.

Our mission is clear: to create a safe and secure online environment for the people of Sri Lanka. We have developed the Spam Catch Sri Lanka project as an open-source initiative hosted on GitHub, where anyone can contribute to our efforts. By uniting our expertise, knowledge, and resources, we aim to build a comprehensive database of spammers and ensure that their harmful activities are exposed.

Our platform serves as a central hub for reporting and identifying spammers operating within Sri Lanka. We encourage individuals and organizations to join us in this crucial fight against privacy invasion. Together, we can bring awareness to the issue, share information, and actively work towards putting an end to spam and unwanted advertising.

Additionally, we understand that online threats extend beyond spamming. Hackers leveraging various platforms to distribute malware, adware, and viruses pose significant risks to users' privacy and security. As part of our commitment to safeguarding your digital well-being, we actively monitor and report such malicious activities, creating a comprehensive and up-to-date repository of threat information.

By building the Spam Catch Sri Lanka project, we aim to empower individuals and communities with the knowledge and tools they need to protect themselves from privacy violations and online threats. Our collective effort will create a safer and more secure digital landscape for everyone.

Join us in our fight against spammers and hackers by contributing to the Spam Catch Sri Lanka project on GitHub. Together, we can build a nation that values privacy, respects user data, and utilizes technology for the betterment of society.

Remember, your voice matters, and together, we can make a difference. Say no to spam and join us at Spam Catch Sri Lanka today!
